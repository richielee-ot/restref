  • Scope: Customers who used RestRef (CHURN_REPORTING_FLAG = 1 AND RESTREF_SEATED_COVERS > 0), analyzing why they churned
  • Product Fit (12.3%) is the #4 churn reason for RestRef users — higher than in prospect losses, suggesting widget issues emerge after onboarding
